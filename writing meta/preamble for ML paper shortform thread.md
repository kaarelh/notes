

# A thread into which I'll occasionally post notes on some ML(?) papers

I think the world would probably be much better if everyone made a bunch more of their notes public. I intend to occasionally copy some personal notes on ML(?) papers into this thread. While I hope that the notes which I'll end up selecting for being posted here will be of interest to some people, and that people will sometimes comment with their thoughts on the same paper and on my thoughts (correct my mistakes, etc.), I expect that the notes here will not be significantly more polished than notes I write for myself / my reasoning will be suboptimal; also, I expect most of these notes won't really make sense unless you also have a paper at hand — the notes will typically be companions to the paper, not substitutes.

So: I'm warning you that the notes in this thread will be messy and not self-contained, and telling you that reading them might not be a good use of your time :)
