

from https://www.reddit.com/r/linguistics/comments/b1oo69/how_old_are_all_the_accepted_proto_languages/ :

"Proto-Uralic is generally seen as being roughly the same age as PIE ~5000-6000 yrs BP

Proto-Dravidian is slightly younger at roughly 5000-4000 years old.

Proto-Niger Congo would have to be at least 6000 years old but it’s probably way older

Proto-Mayan is roughly 3000 years old

Proto-Uto-Aztecan is about 4000-5000 years old

Proto-Nilo-Saharan is at least 10000 years old

Proto Turkic is ~2000 years old

Proto Austronesian is 5000-7000 years old

Proto Na-Dené is at least 7000 years old"
