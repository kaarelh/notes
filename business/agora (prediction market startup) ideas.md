high-level:

1.  Have a separate company (think of this as providing insurance fraud detection) that creates a market that provides insurance on someone's house. I.e. the market is on whether the house will be hit by a tornado, the owner of the house bets on YES and "insurance providers" bet on NO. (We might want to think about whether third parties should also be allowed to bet on YES – I don't see much how it improves much if they aren't, except maybe as a safety mechanism?) Commission is split between the exchange and the market creator, probably with most of the proceeds going to the market creator. This makes insurance publicly traded, probably increasing transparency and efficiency. Insurance companies are currently both providing insurance and detecting fraud – an unnecessary bundling. The insurance providers will need to trust the market creator though; this can hopefully be accomplished with a reputation system for market creators + transparency on the part of the market creator.
2.  Use language model to do resolution, eg have model read the news and say which way a market will go
