map from open disk to open disk
holomorphic, 0 to 0
then |f(z)|<=|z| for all z and equality for any z implies it is a rotation!

pf: consider f(z)/z, apply maximum modulus principle on disks of radii r<1 getting closer, note that this gives |f(z)/z| at most 1/r, which implies always at most 1. if 1 inside somewhere, then it would be a local max, and then the max modulus principle would say the func is const. f(z)/z being const with |f(z)/z|=1 is f(z) being a rotation
