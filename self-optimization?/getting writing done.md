
a soft accountability method:
1) create times at which you will show your writing to someone. ie just agree with a friend/collaborator/coworker to do this at time x every week. and i guess say you'll have something. and you'd have to show what you have either way

can also use this to decide when to finish, should help with being too meticulous. probably would need to write by starting from bullet points, then expanding all a bit, then expanding a bit more, and so on, ie all sections in parallel, for it to make sense for them to say you ought to stop and for you to then stop at some point. or could do this section by section. eg ask the friend to look at a section each week and (commit to?) stop when they say it's good enough, then move on to the next section
