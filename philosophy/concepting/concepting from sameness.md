maybe one should think of concepting in terms of sameness? of considering certain a priori different things the same. abstraction is a gain in that the quotient is genuinely new, but it's a loss in that the new classification is coarser than the finest possible one you can access. eg when you play a melody on the piano and i repeat it on a guitar, we think of ourselves as playing the same thing. the pressure sequences are in fact not the same, and they don't even sound identical to our inner ears post fourier transform, but we can easily have a view where they are the same, anyway.

then there is the standard question of where the quotientings come from. one answer attempt is that they come from similar properties (clusters in thingspace), including similar action-guidance.
