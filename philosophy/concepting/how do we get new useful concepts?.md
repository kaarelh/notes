
# the same question for technologies
i think the following question is similar:
* how do we get new useful technologies?

often, we get a new technology by asking:
* what could i do with method X?
and then noticing one could make a device that does something cool/useful with that method and making that

often, we get a new technology by asking:
* how could we do X?
and coming up with a way to do X and implementing it

of course, really there is often a combination: one keeps in mind various methods and various potential uses — one forward-chains and backward-chains at once
