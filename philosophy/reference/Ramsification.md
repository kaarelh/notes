Suppose you have a physical theory stated in terms of some potentially circular relations, x defined partially in terms of y and y defined partially in terms of x, i.e. stuff maybe leading to Quine's holism. Then can say $\exists x \exists y$ such that x is blabla and y is blabla instead, and the final theory involves a search over all possible objects to find the x, y, and so on that minimize the error made by all propositions in our theory. I guess there are many options for what to pick as the error here. Also, I guess we might need a reference object level to exist independently of theory choice for this to make sense, but it is still pretty cool.
