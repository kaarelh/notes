* the exogeneous components could just all be evil. then you are either stuck not developing or becoming evil
* the components could be 50/50 good/bad but st it's really hard to tell (can probably construct a specific example using time-bounded halting style ideas). then if you only have so much time to assess if the components are good or bad, you're going to be stuck or accepting a lot of bad components

on the other hand it could also be the case that all exogenous components are good though! really, the question is what kinds of good/bad distributions you can generate yourself, and then what kinds you should generate to do the most good, i guess
