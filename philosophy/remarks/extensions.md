
In AI alignment / philosophy, we would like to understand good extensions of our (scientific/understandative/ethical) reasoning systems. There's a bunch of examples of extending things in a bunch of places that might be worth thinking about! For one, in mathematics, there's extensions of particular small objects, e.g. one can take an analytic real function and extend it to a holomorphic (complex) function. There's also more meta things like extending a theory to cover more cases. Of course, in mathematics, all this works out neatly, and we'd want to have something that works a lot less neatly.
