
the elements of a mind relate to each other in language; the elements of a group of people (individual humans) do so, too

this seems like a generally fruitful analogy:
* humanity is a linguistically-structured 'mind' thinking to itself (eg academia) much like an individual is thinking to zerself in a largely(?) linguistic manner
* our mental elements play games that are very much like the games played by elements of societies
* we can see individuals and society as reasoning systems
