
in some cases, to figure out how to reason (e.g. anthropics, e.g. maybe the infinite dice case from caspar hare), ask what kind of reasoning would be good for you; in particular, consider some broader class of agents and ask what kind of reasoning would be good for agents in this class

this can take two flavors:
1) good simpliciter, ethically good (maybe more appropriate for the dice case)
2) helpful in particular for coming to good beliefs about one's universe, for seeing
