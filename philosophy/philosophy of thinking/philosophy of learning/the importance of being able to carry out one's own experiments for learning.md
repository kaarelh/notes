
when learning a language, it seems very good to be able to try stuff and be corrected?

generally, want to be able to "ask questions". like there are particular things which would help you, and these particular things are sometimes unlikely to be in your given static data. and then it's great for you if you can just create that data

also an RL (framing of the) point: you have your particular strategies, and feedback on those strategies is much more useful than feedback on random different strategies from others that you cannot replicate that well anyway. like, on-policy feedback helps!
