	* one can also create structure/structures. it's probably fair to say that most structures we 2025-humans make use of were created by us (examples: technologies, organizations, words/concepts). and many of the remaining structures were created by (other parts of) evolution
	* really, there is structure to the mathematico-possibilistic world, and we keep encountering/discovering more of it.
	* abstract things can end up making themselves felt inside physics.

		* you can think about what the computer is doing by way of thinking about the abstract algorithm
